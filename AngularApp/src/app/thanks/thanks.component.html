Thank You

You rock! Thanks for enrolling as a member at Ithaca Generator.

Here are some next steps to get you started on the path to getting the most out of your membership, and contributing to our awesome community.

Please sign up for our "members only" google group to ask questions or get updates. The link is here: HERE. To test it out, send the group a hello message and tell us a bit about yourself. We're happy to meet you!

Now that you are a member, you can attend the monthly IG classes in any field you are interested in. We have classes in...

  • Welding
  • Woodworking
  • Laser Cut Design
  • Science Fiction book club
  • Electronics Meetups
  • Board Game Nights

We have even more classes in the works- A wide range of topics from coding and virtual reality, to sewing and pottery, are available to explore. Interested in a topic and don't see it as a class or meeting? Reach out! Chances are there are others interested too.

For all upcoming events, and to register for the ones you're interested in, visit the Events Calendar on our website HERE

Questions? Shout them out in response to info@ithacagenerator.org, or to the google group.
