You rock! Thanks for enrolling as a member at Ithaca Generator.

Here are some next steps to get you started on the path to getting the most out of your membership, and contributing to our awesome community.

Please sign up for our "members only" google group to ask questions or get updates. The link is HERE. To test it out, send the group a hello message and tell us a bit about yourself. We're happy to meet you!

Now that you are a member, you can attend the monthly IG classes in any field you are interested in. We regularly schedule classes in:

  • Welding
  • Woodworking
  • Laser cutting of wood, plastic & paper
  • Electronics
  • Ceramics
  • Special classes taught by freelance teachers

For a current list see Upcoming Core Classes and Upcoming Special Classes. Note that as a member you can take core classes for free by entering the coupon code "{{code}}" when you checkout. We also have various clubs and workshops you can attend, such as:

  • Science Fiction book club
  • Board Game Nights
  • Fiber Nights
  • Interactive Art Workshop
  • Model Airplane Club

For a current list see Upcoming Clubs. If you don't see a class or club you would like, or if you have an idea of something you would like to lead, shoot an email to edu@ithacagenerator.org.

For all upcoming events, and to register for the ones you're interested in, visit the Events Calendar on our website HERE

Questions? Shout them out in response to info@ithacagenerator.org, or to the google group.
